# Hot Restaurants Research Sources (Dallas Area)
# These are the ONLY sources to use.

## Primary Sources
- https://dallas.eater.com/ (Eater Dallas heatmap and latest)
- https://www.dmagazine.com/food-dining/ (D Magazine dining)
- https://www.dallasobserver.com/food-drink (Dallas Observer)
- https://www.centraltrack.com/category/dining/ (Central Track Dallas)

## Secondary/Backup
- https://papercitymag.com/dining/ (PaperCity Dallas dining)
- https://www.guidelive.com/food-drink (GuideLive)
- https://www.theinfatuation.com/dallas (Infatuation Dallas)

## Guidelines
- Focus on Dallas proper and immediate suburbs (Highland Park, University Park, Oak Cliff, Deep Ellum, Bishop Arts, Design District, Lower Greenville, Uptown)
- Prioritize NEW openings (within last 2-4 weeks) or places with current buzz
- Include brief backstory/why it's hot
- Capture signature dish or "what to try"
- Note price range: $ = under $15, $$ = $15-30, $$$ = $30-50, $$$$ = $50+
