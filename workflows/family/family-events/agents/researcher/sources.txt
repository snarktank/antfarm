## Guidelines
- Focus on events within ~20 miles of Allen/Plano/Frisco/McKinney
- Include date, time, location, and cost in every listing
- Prioritize free/low-cost events ($0-20 per person)
- Include both registration-required and drop-in events
- Note age recommendations if provided
