# Holiday Research Template

## Five Sections to Research

### 1. Things to do
Activities, traditions, crafts, at-home celebrations

### 2. Places to go
Events, festivals, cultural centers, community gatherings

### 3. Foods & recipes
Traditional dishes, easy family recipes, symbolic foods

### 4. Cultural facts
History, origin, significance, customs, symbolism

### 5. Kid-friendly activities
Age-appropriate ideas, crafts, stories, games

## Suggested Sources
- Wikipedia (cultural facts, history)
- Pinterest (kid activities, crafts)
- Food blogs (recipes)
- Local event calendars (places to go)
- Cultural organization websites

## Output Format
For each section, provide 3-5 bullet items with brief descriptions.
